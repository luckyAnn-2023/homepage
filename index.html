2024 Achievements of the Artificial Intelligence College

Research Progress

The college made significant advancements in research, including:

  • Applied for multiple research projects such as the Henan Electronic Science and Technology University Initiative, Henan Natural Science Foundation, and an International Science and Technology Cooperation project.
  • Organized faculty members to prepare applications for the 2025 National Natural Science Foundation projects.
  • Published numerous high-impact academic papers, with over 20 indexed in SCI/EI and recognized by core journals. Notably, research was featured in the prestigious journal Applied Soft Computing.

"Industry-University-Research" Integration

The college actively fostered integration between academia and industry:

  • Collaborated with institutions such as Henan Provincial People's Hospital, China Electronics (Zhengzhou) Data Industry Company, and iFlytek Central Plains Headquarters.
  • Hosted a "Industry-University-Research-Application" symposium and participated in activities at the National Engineering Laboratory for Big Data Analysis and Application Technology.

Academic Exchanges

Efforts to enhance academic engagement included:

  • Organizing lectures by prominent scholars such as Prof. Liminglu from Zhejiang Normal University, Academician Ren Xiangshi from Kochi University of Technology, and Prof. Chen Dongyi from UESTC.
  • Participating in the China Higher Education Computer Conference.
  • Conducting exchange visits to institutions including Northeast Forestry University and Huayu Xingye Company.

Graduate Education

Key initiatives in graduate education:

  • Completed the application for the Master’s Degree Program in Intelligent Science and Technology.
  • Developed the 2025 graduate admission brochure and conducted two online recruitment sessions.
  • Collaborated with Zhengzhou University of Technology for graduate program promotion.
  • Applied for Henan Graduate Education Quality Engineering Projects and arranged 2024 graduate examination proctoring tasks.

Infrastructure Enhancements

The college significantly improved its research infrastructure by configuring advanced hardware and software systems for new scientific laboratories, providing robust support for research and teaching activities.
